Pasta Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta or bacon 150g
Eggs 3
Grated Parmesan cheese 1 cup
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste

Steps:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook diced pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and mix in grated Parmesan.
  4. Drain spaghetti and immediately toss in egg mixture.
  5. Add cooked pancetta, season with salt and pepper, and mix well.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan and black pepper.
